To delete your account and all related data (save, results, analytics), email trogstore@gmail.com from the device or email your account is registered with. We will delete the data within 30 days.

The game is not designed to collect personal data from children. Google sign-in is optional; without it the game is fully anonymous.

We may update this policy; the current version is always available at this URL. The effective date is shown above.
